The problem

99% of the physical world is unmapped and un-queryable

Street-level reality changes minute to minute. Traditional mapping is slow, sparse, and expensive. We need a living model built from continuous perception.

Reality is an event stream

Think of the world as a stream of geo-tagged frames. The question is not “where is the road”, but “what is happening here right now”.

Our approach

30K phones × 1 FPS = Living World Model

ClawPhones capture frames on-device. Oysterworld indexes each frame into H3 cells. Developers query the live world with a simple API.

Spatial intelligence @ 100m

Events are aggregated into H3 heatmaps so coverage is instantly visible, filterable, and queryable.
